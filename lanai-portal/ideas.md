# Lanai Lifestyle Portal — Design Brainstorm

## Three Approaches

### Approach 1: "Obsidian Atelier"
Dark, editorial luxury. Deep charcoal backgrounds, champagne gold accents, and serif headings. Inspired by high-end fashion houses and private banking portals.
**Probability:** 0.07

### Approach 2: "Ivory Coast" ← CHOSEN
Warm ivory and sand tones with deep forest green accents and gold highlights. Clean, airy, and warm — like a luxury resort lobby. Inspired by Aman Resorts and Six Senses branding.
**Probability:** 0.08

### Approach 3: "Midnight Concierge"
Deep navy and midnight blue with silver and white. Sophisticated and nocturnal — like a private members club at night.
**Probability:** 0.04

---

## Chosen Approach: "Ivory Coast"

**Design Movement:** Warm Luxury Minimalism — the aesthetic of ultra-high-end resort branding.

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth over coldness — ivory, sand, and warm greens instead of cold greys
2. Generous whitespace — content breathes; nothing feels crowded
3. Typography-led hierarchy — Playfair Display for headings, DM Sans for body
4. Subtle gold as the single luxury signal

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#FAF8F4` (warm ivory)
- Foreground: `#1C2B1A` (deep forest)
- Primary: `#2D5A27` (Lanai green)
- Accent: `#C9A84C` (champagne gold)
- Muted: `#E8E3D8` (warm sand)

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric sidebar navigation (left, narrow, dark green) with a wide content area. Not a standard top-nav SaaS layout.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin gold horizontal rules as section dividers
2. Subtle leaf/botanical motif in sidebar
3. Monogram-style "L" logo mark

**Typography System:**
- Display/Headings: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- Body/UI: DM Sans (clean, readable)
- Monospace/Data: JetBrains Mono (for codes, IDs)

**Brand Essence:** The world's most personal travel relationship — for those who expect more than a booking.
**Brand Voice:** Warm, knowing, discreet. Like a trusted friend who happens to know everyone.
- Headline example: "Your next chapter begins here."
- CTA example: "Tell us where you dream of going."

**Signature Brand Color:** Lanai Green `#2D5A27`
