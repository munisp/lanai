Pillar 3: Client Intelligence & Predictive Personalisation

Move from Reactive Service to Proactive Relationship Management

Current State
Client profiles hold static data — preferences, past trips, membership tier.
360° Living Client Profile
Every interaction (WhatsApp, email, proposal, booking, note) enriches the profile automatically in real time.
Preference Inference Engine
LLM analyses historical interactions to infer unstated preferences ("prefers boutique", "always books suites") and surfaces them as insights.
Predictive Trip Triggers
Proactively alerts advisors based on past patterns: "James typically books a winter ski trip in October. No booking yet this year."
Lifetime Value Scoring
Automatic LTV calculation per client, updated after every booking to drive tier management and advisor prioritisation.
Churn Risk Signals
Clients who haven't engaged in X months, or whose sentiment has declined, are flagged automatically for re-engagement.
Benchmark: Personalised AI interactions drive 60% higher repeat rates and 33% higher LTV. Predictive personalisation is the fastest-growing AI segment in luxury hospitality.
