Pillar 5

Connect Lanai's Supplier Network to Live Inventory, Pricing, and Commission Tracking

Current State
Suppliers are stored as static records in the CRM. Bookings, availability checks, and commissions are tracked manually across disparate systems.
Real-World Benchmark
Agencies using integrated booking systems save 12–18 hours per agent per week. Conversion rates lift 20–35% within 90 days of CRM adoption.
GDS Integration
Direct Amadeus/Sabre connectivity. Real-time flight availability, pricing, and booking directly from the CRM. No switching between systems.
Hotel & Villa API Layer
Direct connections to Hotelbeds, Little Emperors, RateHawk, and luxury villa platforms. Live availability and rates surfaced inside Travel Requests.
Automated Commission
Every booking automatically calculates expected commission, tracks payment status, and alerts the finance team on overdue payments.
Supplier Scorecard
After each trip, the system prompts the advisor (and optionally the client) to rate the supplier. Builds a quality-weighted supplier ranking over time.
Preferred Supplier Alerts
When building a proposal, the system highlights Lanai's preferred/contracted suppliers first, maximising margin and ensuring quality control.
